# Executive Summary

This report summarizes key insights derived from the analytics pipeline
and Power BI dashboards.

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows a declining trend over time.
- Health & beauty and watches & gifts are top-performing categories.
- Customer value is concentrated among a small subset of repeat buyers.

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-value categories.
- Improve retention strategies for low-frequency customers.
- Monitor long-term revenue decline and seasonality effects.
